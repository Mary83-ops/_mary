Get to know me!

Hello there! My name is Maryann Nabwire and I am a passionate frontend developer based in Nairobi, Kenya.

I have always been interested in technology and design, which has led me to pursue a career in frontend development. Although I do not have direct work experience, I have completed various online courses and tutorials to build my knowledge and skills in frontend development.

I recently completed a project where I designed and developed a responsive website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Through this project, I gained valuable experience in front-end development, as well as sharpened my problem-solving and time management skills.

What excites me most about frontend development is the ability to create visually appealing and intuitive user interfaces that enhance the user experience. I am passionate about staying up-to-date with the latest design trends and technologies and am always looking for opportunities to learn and grow in the field.

In my next opportunity, I am looking for a role where I can continue to develop my skills and gain hands-on experience working on exciting projects with a collaborative team. I am excited to bring my enthusiasm and eagerness to learn to your team.
